About

"MemorySwap" suggests a platform where users can exchange or trade items that hold sentimental value or memories. The term combines "memory," referring to the emotional significance of the items, with "swap," indicating the act of exchanging or trading. It conveys the idea of sharing cherished possessions with others who may appreciate them, creating a meaningful and reciprocal experience.
